About Us

discover BccG

BccG is a concept proposed by us based on the popular block chain in recent years. By combining block chain with games, it can play a role in future games. Through the characteristics of decentralization, it can realize anonymous transactions in games with transactions and protect the privacy of players.

Our team used this as a benchmark to design a timely card-type game that fits in with the blockchain and tries to give players a safer and more interesting gaming experience.
